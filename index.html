Sprokbook gives your analog camera digital powers — the when, where, and what of every frame you shoot.

Shoot, Tap, Advance

One tap captures everything

One tap captures the time and GPS coordinates. Include a reference photo so you can match frames to negatives when your scans come back. Every exposure is timestamped, located, and ready to cross-reference.

Every camera, at a glance

Know what's loaded

Forgot whether you loaded Portra 400 or 800? Sprokbook tracks your cameras, the roll in each one, and how far through it you are. Switch between bodies mid-shoot or load a new roll without losing track.

Your shooting history

Every roll, stock, and frame

Scroll back through months of shooting and see the full history — which stocks you've been running, where you've been shooting, how your habits have changed. Your shooting life, organized by camera and roll.

Stay in the moment

Log without breaking your flow

Instantly log exposures from the Action button and your Apple Watch with a Shortcut. Log without opening your app or even picking up your phone.

Your data is portable

Export everything

Save to CSV or JSON for analysis, archiving, or scripting. Everything — location, timestamp, film stock, frame number — comes with you.
